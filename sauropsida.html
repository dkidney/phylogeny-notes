2.0.0.0.5 Parareptilia

End Carboniferous to Tr-J extinction

Includes:

  • mesosaurs (first reptiles to return to marine ecosystems)
  • bolosaurids (first bipedal reptiles)
  • nycteroleterids (the first reptiles with advanced hearing systems)
  • pareiasaurs (first large herbivorous reptiles)
  • procolophonoids (the only parareptiles to survive into the Triassic)
